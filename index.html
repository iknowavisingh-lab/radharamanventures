About Radha Raman Ventures

Radha Raman Ventures is a dependable manpower and gig workforce solutions provider based in Dhanbad, Jharkhand, serving businesses across East India. We specialize in supplying skilled, semi-skilled, unskilled, and gig workforce tailored to diverse industry requirements. Our strength lies in our commitment to compliance, transparency, and workforce reliability. Every engagement is managed with strict adherence to labor laws, safety standards, and operational best practices, ensuring complete peace of mind for our clients. With a deep understanding of regional workforce dynamics and a growing talent network, we enable organizations to scale efficiently, reduce operational risks, and maintain uninterrupted productivity. At Radha Raman Ventures, we don’t just supply manpower — we build long-term workforce partnerships founded on trust, accountability, and performance.
